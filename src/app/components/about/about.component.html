About

I build the same kind of system twice on purpose — once on .NET, once on Spring Boot — to stay fluent across both major backend ecosystems as I move toward designing systems as independent, well-defined services rather than a single monolith.

I'm especially drawn to backend structure — how a system gets broken into services, how those services talk to each other, and how Kubernetes handles the orchestration underneath: deployment, scaling, and service discovery.
